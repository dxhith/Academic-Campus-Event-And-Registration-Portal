About the Event

NexaHack 2025 is the flagship annual hackathon of the university, organized by the Innovation & Entrepreneurship Cell. Now in its 5th edition, this 72-hour marathon challenge invites students from all departments to form teams of 2–5 and build working prototypes for real-world problems.

This year's themes include AI for Social Good, Smart Cities, HealthTech, and EdTech. Teams will be mentored by industry professionals throughout the event.

Who Can Participate?

  • Open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students
  • Teams of 2–5 members (cross-department encouraged)
  • No prior hackathon experience required
  • Must hold valid university ID

What to Bring

  • Laptop and charger
  • University ID card
  • Enthusiasm to build!

Food, beverages, and workstations will be provided. The venue will be open 24/7 during the event duration.
